Email / CV / Google Scholar

Hi, I am a second-year CS PhD student at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Bharath Hariharan and Kavita Bala, focusing on vision, LLM agent, code generation, and deep learning algorithms. My research goal lies in building robust, trustworthy, and interpretable AI system.

Before joining Cornell, I obtained my Medical Doctor degree 🩺 from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU) in Taiwan. I am fortunate to work with Dr. Pin-Yu Chen at MIT-IBM Research, Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu and Prof. Li-Fen Chen at NYCU, and Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang at NTU
